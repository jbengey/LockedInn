An Immsersive VR Experience.

Locked Inn is a VR pub experience created to allow individuals to feel immersed in a pub environment, as covid-19 restrictions (lock down) don't allow them to do so in person.

Pub Features

Our traditional pub is packed full of features for you to explore!

Continuous Movement

A simple locomotion system allows you to explore every aspect of the pub.

Left controller analog stick: Move
Right controller analog stick: Rotate

Full PBR Enviroment

A classic pub environment recreated with full implantation of PBR texturing.

With custom assets and atmospheric lighting supporting the feel of a real pub.

Throwable Glass

Every beer and wine glass found around the pub can be interacted with.

Fancy being reckless? Throw a glass and watch it break into pieces.

Interactable NPC's

Bust a move with some of the pub regulars.

Functioning Fruit Machines

Try your luck on the pub fruit machines.

Darts & Jukebox

Enjoy the varied music or throw a dart.
